Gym Booking System



Definiton

A booking system ensures that the gym facilities are used wisely by scheduling workout sessions. This prevents overcrowding and gives fair access to students and staff. The system provides data on gym usage, helping administrators make informed decisions about improving facilities, adjusting hours, and tailoring fitness programs.
