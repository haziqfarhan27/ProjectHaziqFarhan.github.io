Hotel Reservation System



Definiton

A hotel reservation system is computer software that helps hotels manage guest bookings. It keeps track of available rooms, guest details, and reservations. The system simplifies tasks like check-ins, check-outs, and pricing management. Its main purposes are to improve efficiency, enhance customer service, and optimize revenue by effectively handling hotel operations.
