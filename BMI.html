BMI Calculator



Definiton

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a measure that compares a person's weight to their height. BMI is a commonly used screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight status categories, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity. Here is formula for BMI calculation


BMI = Weight (In Kilogram)/Height(In Meters)*Height(In Meters)
